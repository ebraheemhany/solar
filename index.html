Decision Support System — Analysis Results
AI-Assisted

The DSS integrates spatial analysis, demand forecasting, and radiation suitability models to guide optimal expansion of El Shorouk City's solar infrastructure. Recommendations are derived from satellite imagery (NDVI, LST), population density layers, and historical energy consumption data.

Best Locations for Future Solar Farms

Multi-criteria spatial analysis using GIS overlay of land suitability, proximity to grid, slope analysis, and cloud cover data identifies three high-priority expansion zones in El Shorouk's eastern and southern districts.
